# Delete your RufusFox account and data

This page explains how to delete your **RufusFox** account and all associated data.
Google Play requires this information to be available on the web, including for
people who have already uninstalled the app.

## What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

- your **account** (email / sign-in) from Firebase Authentication;
- all your **synced data** in the cloud — tasks, lists, water, medication and
  settings, and every backup snapshot;
- the **local copy** on the device you delete from.

This is **permanent and cannot be undone.** There is no separate retention period
for deleted account data — it is removed as part of the deletion. (Crash and
feedback reports, if you ever sent any, are kept at most 12 months and are not
linked to a live account after deletion.)

## Option 1 — delete it yourself in the app (fastest)

1. Open RufusFox and make sure you are **signed in**.
2. Go to **Settings → Account**.
3. Tap **"Delete account & data"**.
4. Confirm (you will be asked to type `DELETE`).

Your account and data are deleted and the app returns to the sign-in screen.

## Option 2 — request deletion by email

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot sign in, email
**rufusfox.support@gmail.com** from the address associated with your account (or
tell us the email you used), with the subject **"Delete my account"**. We will
delete your account and data and confirm by reply.
